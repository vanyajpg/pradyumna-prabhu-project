TATTVA-JIJÑĀSĀ

Kāmasya nendriyaprītir lābho jīveta yāvatā
Jīvasya tattvajijñāsā nārtho yaś ceha karmabhiḥ.
(Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam 1.2.10)

A humble attempt to present traditional texts to assist seekers in ascertaining the Way.
